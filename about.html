Local music,

Lyric & Audio Visualizer turns audio, artwork and timed captions from your device into a responsive radial player.

How it works

Choose a browser-supported audio file, optional background and centre artwork, then paste lyrics or open a TXT/SRT file. Circular and horizontal seekers control the same playback position; SRT captions follow the current time.

Private by design

Audio, images and lyric files are processed in your browser and are never uploaded by this tool. Media object URLs exist only for the active page. Theme choices, layout preferences, text metadata, lyrics and a non-media position may be stored in this browser using localStorage; reset them in Edit settings or clear site data.

Accessible motion and controls

The centre playback control and seekers support keyboard focus and meaningful slider values. The interface provides visible focus states and status announcements. Reduced-motion preferences suppress artwork rotation and decorative seeker motion.

Quick start

  1. Open Edit and choose audio.
  2. Add artwork, metadata and lyrics if wanted.
  3. Pick one of twelve themes and independent fonts.
  4. Play, seek and adjust the layout. Your local settings restore next time.
